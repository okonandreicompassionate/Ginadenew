About Oinia Elementary

Founded in 1987 · 420+ happy students · 8-acre green campus
Our Story: Oinia Elementary began as a dream to build a school where every child feels valued and inspired. Over the decades, we've grown into a thriving community where innovation meets tradition, and learning feels like play.
  • Experienced, caring educators
  • Small class sizes for personal attention
  • Modern classrooms and STEM labs
  • Creative arts, music, and sports programs
  • Support services for all learners
  • Active family and community partnerships
From the Principal: Ms. Emily Carter
“At Oinia, we believe in the power of curiosity and kindness. Our teachers are dedicated to creating a safe, joyful space where children can discover their strengths, ask big questions, and make lifelong friends. We invite families to join us on this journey and help every child shine.”
Founder: Mr. Thomas Oinia
“We started Oinia Elementary with a vision: to create a school where learning is joyful, every child is known, and talents are celebrated. Our mission continues today, as we grow, adapt, and nurture the next generation of dreamers and doers.”
