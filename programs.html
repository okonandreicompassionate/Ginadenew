Oinia Elementary Roadmap of Activities & Programs
Academic Kickoff
September 2
Start the year with themed assemblies, classroom orientations, and buddy welcome activities. Each grade receives a roadmap of learning goals and fun challenges.
Art & Music Festival
October 6
A week of creative workshops, daily music performances, and a student gallery. Visiting artists and musicians inspire every child to create their own masterpiece.
Sports & Wellness Month
November
Daily fitness challenges, team games, and nutrition workshops. Family Field Day, yoga mornings, and “Walk to School” week promote a healthy lifestyle.
Wellbeing & Kindness Week
December
Daily mindfulness exercises, kindness challenges, and support circles. School counselors lead workshops on positive relationships and emotional health.
Community & Clubs Launch
January
New clubs kick off: robotics, gardening, drama, chess, and more. Parent volunteers and teachers host leadership workshops and service projects.
Spring STEM Showcase
March
Science fair, coding contests, and engineering design challenges. Families explore interactive exhibits and student inventions.
End-of-Year Carnival
June
School-wide celebration with games, performances, art booths, and a talent show. Families and teachers honor student achievements and growth.
