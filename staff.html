Meet Our Staff

Ms. Emily Carter
Principal
With over 25 years in education, Ms. Carter is the heart of Oinia Elementary. She believes every child deserves a champion and builds a culture of respect, joy, and curiosity. Outside of school, she enjoys hiking, painting, and volunteering at local animal shelters.
Fun fact: She once climbed Mount Kilimanjaro!
Email: emily.carter@oinia.edu
Mr. John Nguyen
Grade 4 Teacher
Mr. Nguyen is known for his hands-on science experiments and interactive math games. His classroom is a hub of excitement and discovery. An advocate for STEM, he runs the after-school robotics club and loves to cook and travel with his family.
Fun fact: He collects rare comic books and speaks three languages.
Email: john.nguyen@oinia.edu
Ms. Sarah Lopez
Art & Music Teacher
Ms. Lopez fills Oinia with music, color, and creativity. Her art classes are vibrant and her music lessons are always full of laughter. She organizes the annual school play, and loves teaching kids about world cultures through art.
Fun fact: She plays five musical instruments.
Email: sarah.lopez@oinia.edu
Mrs. Linda Smith
School Counselor
Mrs. Smith is the go-to for big feelings and small worries. She teaches mindfulness, resilience, and kindness in every lesson. After hours, she leads family workshops and volunteers at a crisis helpline.
Fun fact: She’s a certified yoga instructor!
Email: linda.smith@oinia.edu
Mr. David Brown
Physical Education
Mr. Brown makes fitness fun for everyone. His energetic games and sports clinics help kids discover their strengths and build confidence. He organizes the annual Field Day, and runs weekend hiking trips for families.
Fun fact: He once ran a marathon dressed as a superhero!
Email: david.brown@oinia.edu
Mr. Alex Turner
School Librarian
Mr. Turner brings stories to life in the Oinia library. He runs reading challenges and book fairs, and always has a recommendation for every student. He also coaches the chess club and loves photography.
Fun fact: He’s written a children’s mystery novel!
Email: alex.turner@oinia.edu
