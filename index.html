About Oinia Elementary

Oinia Elementary is a vibrant, inclusive community for ages 5-12. We blend academics, social-emotional learning, arts, and play. Our campus has modern classrooms and safe outdoor spaces.

  • Certified, caring educators
  • Small class sizes
  • STEM, arts, and PE programs
  • Support services for all learners
  • Fun events all year!
